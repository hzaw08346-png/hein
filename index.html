Who We Are

Since 2005, We love to keep aquarium more than just a hobby, we see aquarium keeping as a way to connect with nature, to bring a slice of the ocean or a glimpse of a freshwater stream into our homes. We strive to promote responsible aquarium practices, emphasizing the well-being of our aquatic inhabitants and advocating for the protection of their natural habitats.

How Do We Breed

Breeding aquarium life is a rewarding experience that offers insights into aquatic life cycles. It ranges from simple, natural spawning to complex, selective breeding. Success depends on understanding the specific needs of each species, including water parameters, diet, and spawning conditions. Whether it's witnessing new life or contributing to conservation, breeding requires patience, observation, and a willingness to learn.
